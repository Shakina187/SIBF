    Company Secretary (CS)

    The Company Secretary (CS) course is a prestigious professional program designed for individuals aspiring to become experts in corporate laws, governance, compliance, and company administration. This course builds strong legal and managerial foundations essential for corporate decision-making and regulatory compliance in modern business organizations.

    The CS program offers a structured learning path covering all three stages — CSEET (Executive Entrance Test), CS Executive, and CS Professional — ensuring comprehensive preparation to become a qualified Company Secretary.

    Syllabus

    Business Communication

    Professional communication, writing skills, and corporate correspondence.

    Company Law

    Corporate legislation, governance frameworks, and compliance requirements.

    Economic & Commercial Laws

    Understanding economic policies, commercial regulations, and trade laws.

    Securities Laws & Capital Markets

    SEBI regulations, capital market operations, and investor protection.

    Corporate Governance

    Board practices, ethics, compliance management, and risk governance.

    Tax Laws & Practice

    Direct and indirect tax laws applicable to corporate entities.
